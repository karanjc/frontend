ABOUT US

                            Football is considered the world's greatest sport and "the beautiful game".There is the beauty of the goals scored - through free-kicks, open play and out-witting several defenders single-handedly - as well as the artistry of the build-up play, the final pass, the link-up of players combining together to put it in the back of the net. The skilled mastery of football legends such as Johan Cruyff, Pele, Zinedine Zidane, Ronaldinho and Diego Mardona only add to the sport's allure. The manner in which fantastic goals are scored are also part of its charm - volleys, chips, lobs and headers.
